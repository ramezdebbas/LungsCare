Lungs Care:
Description:
Lungs Care is an app that guides about how to cure from Lungs Cancer, how to avoid from it, the best way of treatment, exams, Best tests for it, the main causes of Lungs Cancer and symptoms of Lungs Care.

Features:
•	Fully medicated research based application on Lungs Cancer(also recommended by Doctors)
•	The Research results on the causes of Lungs Cancer.
•	The best ways of treatment of Lungs Cancer.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
